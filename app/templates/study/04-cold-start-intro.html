Thank you for completing the background questionnaire.

In the following section, we will present you with a series of movies. For each of these movies, indicate whether you either have watched and enjoyed the movie or, if you have not watched the movie, whether you would be interested in it.

You can use the buttons provided to indicate your choice.
Alternatively you can use the E key to state that you are not interested in the movie (Exclude) or the I key to indicate that you are interested (Include).
If you are on a touch device, you can also swipe an item to the right to indicate interest and to the left to indicate that you are not interested.

Please do not think to long on a choice but rather choose based on your first intuition
